= amanzi

This is a testbed for redmine extensions of interest to Amanzi

Current features
* Macros for the wiki:
** Toolbar of child pages (extension of child_pages macro)
** Toolbar and list of version wiki pages (wiki navigation of versions)
** Templates
** Embed YouTube videos
** Embed Vimeo videos
** Embed Photowidget

Things to consider:
* Macro for adding raphael graphs to wiki pages
* Macro for adding graphviz content directly in wiki pages
